Holders are ranked based on their total score rating, calculated by summing the score of all NFTs in their wallet.

  • Top-Tier Rewards: The highest-ranking holder will receive the grand prize, which could include exclusive NFTs, merchandise, or high-value rewards.

The higher your total score rating, the higher your chances of receiving exclusive NFT airdrops that are not available for sale.

  • Airdrops may include rare, limited-edition NFTs as well as tokens that can be used within the project ecosystem or traded.

3. Revenue Sharing Opportunities

As a way to give back to our most loyal supporters, top holders may receive a share of the project's royalty earnings or a percentage of proceeds from future collections.

4. Priority in Future Drops

The higher your score, the better your position in the whitelist or early access for future NFT collections, ensuring you can expand your collection before others.

5. Community Influence

Top-rated holders will have a voice in shaping the future of the project through voting rights on major decisions like new themes, collaborations, or utility expansions.

The Key to Success:

Collect and hold as many NFTs as possible to boost your total score rating.

Remember, quality matters too—NFTs with higher individual scores can significantly impact your total score.

Start building your collection and aim for the top of the leaderboard. The rewards await!
