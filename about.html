What is FlintFrames?

FlintFrames is an innovative NFT art collection inspired by the Stone Age, brought to life through captivating 2D cartoon-style animation. FlintFrames is a humorous, non-speaking cartoon animation series on YouTube, bringing entertainment to every viewer while building a vibrant community for NFT art enthusiasts. But FlintFrames is more than just an art project—it’s an ecosystem where creativity meets engagement.
